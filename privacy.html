Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 4, 2025

Welcome to Yumckify ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website, Yumckify.com (the "Site"), and when using our services. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit our Site, interact with our content, participate in our community, or use any of our services. By accessing or using the Site, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the Site.

We understand that your personal information is important to you, and we take our responsibility to protect it very seriously. This policy is designed to be as clear and comprehensive as possible, explaining our practices in detail so you can make informed decisions about your data. We regularly review our privacy practices to ensure they align with industry best practices and applicable legal requirements. Your trust is paramount to us, and we strive to be transparent about how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information from and about users of our Site, including information that may directly or indirectly identify you ("personal information"). The types of information we collect depend on how you interact with our Site and services.

1.1. Information You Provide to Us Directly

  • Account Registration Information: When you create an account on Yumckify, we collect information such as your username, email address, password, and any profile information you choose to provide (e.g., profile picture, bio, preferred cooking styles, dietary preferences).
  • Content and Interaction Information: If you upload recipes, participate in food challenges, post comments, engage in forums, or use other interactive features, we collect the content you submit and information about your interactions. This includes text, images, videos, and metadata associated with your contributions.
  • Communications: When you communicate with us directly, such as through email, customer support inquiries, feedback forms, or social media, we collect the content of your communications and any contact information you provide.
  • Survey and Contest Information: If you participate in surveys, contests, or promotions, we may collect information you provide, such as your name, contact details, demographic information, and responses to survey questions.
  • Shopping and Transaction Information: If you make purchases through our "Shop the Yum" section, we collect information necessary to process your order, including shipping address, billing address, payment information (though actual payment processing is handled by third-party processors and we do not store full credit card details), and purchase history.
  • Newsletter Subscriptions: If you subscribe to our newsletters or marketing communications, we collect your email address and preferences.
  • Dietary and Allergy Information (Optional): If you choose to provide it, we may collect information about your dietary restrictions, allergies, or culinary preferences to personalize your experience and recipe recommendations. This information is highly sensitive and treated with the utmost care.

1.2. Information Collected Automatically

When you access and use the Site, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing activity. This information helps us understand how our users interact with our Site, improve our services, and ensure the security of our platform.

  • Device Information: We collect information about the device you use to access the Site, including your IP address, device type (e.g., mobile, tablet, desktop), operating system, browser type and version, language settings, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Information: We collect details of your visits to our Site, including traffic data, location data, logs, and other communication data and the resources that you access and use on the Site. This includes pages viewed, time spent on pages, clickstream data, search queries, and referring/exit pages.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies, web beacons, pixels, and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your browsing behavior. These technologies help us remember your preferences, analyze trends, administer the website, track users’ movements around the website, and gather demographic information about our user base as a whole. For more detailed information, please refer to our Cookies Policy.
  • Location Information: We may collect general location information (e.g., country or city) based on your IP address. With your consent, we may also collect more precise location data from your mobile device if you enable location services for our app or website.

1.3. Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from third-party sources, such as social media platforms, analytics providers, and advertising partners, to supplement the information we collect directly.

  • Social Media: If you choose to link your Yumckify account with a social media account (e.g., Facebook, Instagram, TikTok), or if you interact with our social media pages, we may receive information from those platforms in accordance with their privacy policies and your privacy settings on those platforms. This may include your public profile information, friend lists, and other data you have authorized the platform to share.
  • Analytics Providers: We use third-party analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics) that collect information about your use of the Site and report it back to us. This data is typically aggregated and anonymized.
  • Advertising Partners: We may receive information from our advertising partners to help us measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns and to deliver more relevant ads to you.

    • To understand how users interact with our Site and services.

    • To send you newsletters, marketing communications, and promotional offers that we believe may be of interest to you (you can opt-out at any time).
    • To notify you about changes to our terms, policies, or services.
    • To respond to your comments, questions, and requests.
  • For Security and Fraud Prevention:
    • To protect the security and integrity of our Site and services.
    • To detect, prevent, and investigate fraudulent or unauthorized activities.
    • To enforce our Terms of Service and other policies.
  • For Legal Compliance:
    • To comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or governmental requests.
    • To establish, exercise, or defend our legal rights.

5. Data Security

The security of your personal information is paramount to us. We implement a variety of technical and organizational measures designed to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption: We use industry-standard encryption (e.g., SSL/TLS) to protect data transmitted between your browser and our servers.
  • Access Controls: Access to personal information is restricted to authorized personnel who need the information to perform their job functions.
  • Regular Security Audits: We regularly review our security practices and systems to identify and address potential vulnerabilities.
  • Data Minimization: We only collect personal information that is necessary for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy.
  • Data Storage: Personal information is stored on secure servers with robust security features.
  • Incident Response Plan: We have procedures in place to respond to potential data breaches or security incidents promptly and effectively.

Despite our best efforts, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your personal information. You also play a crucial role in protecting your data by choosing a strong, unique password for your Yumckify account and keeping it confidential. Do not share your password with anyone. If you suspect any unauthorized access to your account, please contact us immediately.

6. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location and applicable data protection laws (such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in the European Union or the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) in California), you may have certain rights regarding your personal information. These rights may include:

  • Right to Access: You have the right to request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You have the right to request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to Be Forgotten"): In certain circumstances, you have the right to request the deletion of your personal information.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal information in certain situations.
  • Right to Data Portability: You have the right to receive your personal information in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format and to transmit that data to another controller, where technically feasible.
  • Right to Object: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal information in certain circumstances, including for direct marketing purposes.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where we rely on your consent to process your personal information, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time. This will not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority if you believe your data protection rights have been violated.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the contact details provided in the "Contact Us" section below. We will respond to your request in accordance with applicable law. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling your request.

13.2. For California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) grants you specific rights regarding your personal information. This section supplements the information contained in the rest of our Privacy Policy and applies solely to visitors, users, and others who reside in the State of California.

  • Right to Know: You have the right to request that we disclose what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell.
  • Right to Delete: You have the right to request the deletion of your personal information collected or maintained by us, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: Yumckify does not sell your personal information. We do not disclose personal information to third parties for monetary or other valuable consideration.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: You have the right not to receive discriminatory treatment for exercising any of your CCPA rights.

To exercise your CCPA rights, please submit a verifiable consumer request to us by contacting us via the details in the "Contact Us" section. Only you, or a person registered with the California Secretary of State that you authorize to act on your behalf, may make a verifiable consumer request related to your personal information. The verifiable consumer request must:

  1. Provide sufficient information that allows us to reasonably verify you are the person about whom we collected personal information or an authorized representative.
  2. Describe your request with sufficient detail that allows us to properly understand, evaluate, and respond to it.

We cannot respond to your request or provide you with personal information if we cannot verify your identity or authority to make the request and confirm the personal information relates to you. We will only use personal information provided in a verifiable consumer request to verify the requestor's identity or authority to make the request.
